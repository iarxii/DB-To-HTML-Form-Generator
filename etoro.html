U.S. Holiday Retail Sales Increase

Holiday season sales in U.S. retailers rose 3.1% year-over-year, driven by strong online and in-store purchases. Apparel and grocery sectors saw growth, while electronics and jewelry dipped slightly. Restaurant spending surged by 7.8%.

The crypto comeback

In 2023, Bitcoin and Ethereum dramatically outperformed traditional assets, with increases of 167% and 91% respectively. Meanwhile, the Nasdaq, S&P 500, and gold saw more modest gains, while oil and commodities experienced a 10%-12% decline, underscoring cryptocurrencies' standout performance.

U.S. Economic Stability

2023 ended with U.S. inflation aligning with the Federal Reserve's 2% target, a sign of economic stability post-pandemic. The robust job market and strong consumer spending defied recession fears, hinting at a successful 'soft landing' strategy by Fed Chair Jerome Powell.
